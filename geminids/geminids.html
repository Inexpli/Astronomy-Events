The Geminids are the strongest meteor shower of the year. However in 2021, the peak falls on the night of Monday, December 13, when the moon will be nearly full, making dark sky conditions hard to find. It is considered by many to be the best shower in the heavens, producing up to 120 multicolored meteors per hour at its peak. The entire shower lasts from December 4 to 17, 2021.
